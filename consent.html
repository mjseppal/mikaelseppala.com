Consent Service Systems Design

CLIENT: Large Mobile Operator




Tools and methods used
Contextual interviews
Personas
Ideation workshop
Service Blueprinting
UI prototyping
System Maps

Topics
Consent Management
Master Data Management
Identity and Access Management

The project was part of a deep-dive into the consent/authorization issues related to the mobile operator’s business. My role was to be a facilitator for the design process.

During the project’s problem phase I interviewed consumers to understand how they feel about mobile operator consent issues and how they would like them to be solved. Different types of personnel (shop sellers, channel developers, MDM and GDPR experts, enterprise architects and user interface designers) were also interviewed and involved to uncover the intricate makings and related projects that could affect the consent issues.

The project’s solution phase included an ideation workshop, a service blueprinting workshop and a UI prototyping workshop with the mobile operator’s experts. The deliverables also included system maps to visualize how different components should work together to enable the consent service system.
